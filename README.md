**Description:**
Setting: Alone on a Planet, out in the Galaxy, Foggy.
The game is about an expedition ship that has crashed on the planet Gamma Centauri. You are an android responsible for maintenance near the crash site, which is why the military contacts you and orders you to assist in the investigation of the crash, since the **stupid** AI of the research rovers that are supposed to handle the investigation keeps causing accidents. So you set out to repair the rovers and uncover the circumstances of the catastrophe.

**Controls:**
WASD - Movement
Space - Jump 
F - Flashlight
E - Interact 
Mouse to play the Minigames
